# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors and users of nestjs-authentication-worker.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and inclusive of all perspectives
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being patient and constructive in discussions
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or bullying
- Offensive comments related to personal characteristics
- Trolling or insulting comments
- Unwelcome sexual attention
- Any form of abuse

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards. Violations should be reported to the project maintainers.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces and community interactions.
